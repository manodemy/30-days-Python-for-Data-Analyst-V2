Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the absolute minimum data required to facilitate safe course access, verify payments, and enable interactive compiler sandbox progress tracking:

  • Account Credentials: Email address and name provided during manual credentials signup or via single-sign-on (Google OAuth).
  • Payment Details: Processed fully securely by PCI-DSS compliant gateways (Razorpay, Stripe, PayPal). We never store or capture credit card data on our servers.
  • Usage & Progress Metadata: Solved interview coding challenges, daily notebook completions, activity telemetry timestamps, and score milestones.
  • Anonymized Geolocation: Country codes resolved via IP lookup to present localized currencies and pricing structures correctly.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your user metadata is strictly protected and used exclusively to service your browser workspace:

  • To initialize and authorize active interactive browser notebooks.
  • To process checkout transactions and prevent duplicate billing conflicts.
  • To render course milestones, leaderboards, and completion certificates.
  • To distribute order confirmation emails and essential platform updates.

3. Payment Security & Compliance

All checkouts are managed using end-to-end tokenized workflows. Transaction validation is executed via secure backend Supabase Edge Functions. We maintain zero access to payment card numbers or processing authorization details.

4. Cloud Security & Storage

Your information is stored inside secure cloud nodes operated by Supabase (under AWS virtualization layers). Access control is dynamically isolated on a per-user basis using row-level security (RLS) policies.

5. Third-Party Integrations

To provide a frictionless hands-on compiler sandbox, we sync with the following premium partners:

  • Supabase: Auth management, telemetry storage, and course progress databases.
  • Razorpay / Stripe / PayPal: Secure, encrypted currency payment checkouts.
  • GeoJS: Anonymized geolocating to compute appropriate regional pricing.

6. Your Rights & Data Export

You retain full control over your active workspace. You can request a complete copy of your progress records or request permanent account deletion by contacting us directly.

7. Inquiries & Contact

For any privacy or technical compliance concerns, please email our engineering team at manodemy25@gmail.com.
