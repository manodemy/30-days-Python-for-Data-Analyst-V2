1. Conditional Aggregation : COUNTIF, SUMIF, AVERAGEIF

🔍 Concept
COUNTIF(range, criteria) counts cells meeting a condition. SUMIF(range, criteria, sum_range) sums cells in a sum_range where the criteria range matches. AVERAGEIF(range, criteria, avg_range) averages matching values. The multi-condition versions COUNTIFS, SUMIFS, and AVERAGEIFS accept multiple range/criteria pairs.

📊 Day 09 Executive Summary

SUMIF/COUNTIF/AVERAGEIF are the conditional aggregation trinity in Excel. For multiple conditions, use the IFS variants: SUMIFS(sum_range, range1, crit1, range2, crit2). These functions power virtually all business reporting in Excel — revenue by region, count by status, average by category.
